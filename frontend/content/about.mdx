While earning my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida, I built a strong foundation in programming and software engineering principles. It gave me a theoretical and practical base for building well-structured and maintainable systems.

Projects such as the SET Team Dashboard and Log Crawler helped the SET team monitor and debug low-level software more effectively, improving testing accuracy and turnaround time on issue resolution.  

Currently, at Designer Eyes, I focus on enhancing and maintaining our internal data infrastructure and E-Commerce systems. I’ve scaled our database architecture and ETL pipelines to improve data reliability and enable greater automation across departments. These improvements have laid the foundation for expanding into new channels and markets, while also supporting seamless integration of third-party and in-house software solutions.
